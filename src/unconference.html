conan Unconference
September 26-27, 2022 | Madrid, Spain
Join the Conan Team and a select group of the Conan Tribe for our first official Conan Unconference. We are back in person and ready to network and discuss the latest and greatest in the Conan C/C++ community. The Conan Unconference is two days with an agenda that you help create. No slides, just suggested topics voted on by everyone in the room and interactive, group sessions built off of that. We also have afternoon activities planned and a group dinner for the first night. See the agenda below.
